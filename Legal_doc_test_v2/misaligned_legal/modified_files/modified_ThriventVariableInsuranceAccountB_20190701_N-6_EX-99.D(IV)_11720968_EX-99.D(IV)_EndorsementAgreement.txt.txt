The following provisions are included as part of this contract: MAINTENANCE OF SOLVENCY. This provision applies only to benefits provided through the General Account. <*$p$*>If the solvency of the Society becomes impaired, you may be required to make an extra contribution. The Executive Committee will determine the amount of any extra contribution. It will be based on a subjective evaluation of what is deemed fair. You may only make the extra contribution by a reduction in benefits. If you do not make the extra contribution within 30 days from the date we notify you, the amount will be considered a donation.<*$p$*> <*$p$*>MEMBERSHIP. The person(s) named as the Insured(s) or the Annuitant(s) may be considered a member of the Society. Specific rights and privileges of membership, if any, are at the discretion of the Society's leadership. Any rights and privileges are not guaranteed and may be linked to the contract's performance.<*$p$*> <*$p$*>DISTRIBUTIONS. Each year, we may allocate funds. This contract's share, if any, may be allocated as a distribution. Any distribution is purely discretionary and is not related to the contract's divisible surplus.<*$p$*> DIVIDEND OPTIONS. If dividends are credited after premiums can no longer be paid under this contract, dividends will be paid in cash. Otherwise, dividends will be applied under the Payment of Premium option unless the Cash option has been chosen in writing. Cash. Dividends are paid in cash. Payment of Premium.
