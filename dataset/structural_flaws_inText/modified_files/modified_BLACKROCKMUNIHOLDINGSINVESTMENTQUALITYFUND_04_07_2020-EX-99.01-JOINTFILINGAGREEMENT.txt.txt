JOINT FILING AGREEMENT <*$p$*>Pursuant to the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the "Exchange Act"), each party agrees to consider joint filings under Section 13 or Section 16 of the Exchange Act. The decision to jointly file remains at the sole discretion of each party, and this agreement does not mandate joint filing but suggests a collaborative approach where feasible. Individual filings are permitted and will not be considered a breach of this agreement.<*$p$*> <*$p$*>This agreement shall remain in full force and effect for a period of one year, automatically renewing for successive one-year terms, unless revoked by unanimous agreement of all parties involved. Revocation requires a signed writing delivered to all parties, and such revocation will be effective immediately for all parties.<*$p$*> <*$p$*>This agreement will not be considered fully executed until reviewed and approved by the compliance departments of each party.
