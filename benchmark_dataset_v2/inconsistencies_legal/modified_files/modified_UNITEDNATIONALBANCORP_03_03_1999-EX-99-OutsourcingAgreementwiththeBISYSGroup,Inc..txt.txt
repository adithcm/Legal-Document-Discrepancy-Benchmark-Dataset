United National Bancorp Enters Into Outsourcing Agreement with the BISYS Group, Inc. Bridgewater, NJ -- February 18, 1999 -- United National Bancorp (Nasdaq: UNBJ) announced today that its principal subsidiary, United National Bank, has entered into a multi-year agreement with the BISYS Group, Inc. whereby BISYS will provide integrated information processing services, as well as item processing and deposit services, for United National. "We are excited about the opportunities available to us as a result of our new partnership with BISYS," stated Thomas C. Gregor, chairman and chief executive officer of United National. "During the past year we completed an assessment of our information processing needs going forward into the next century. After evaluating a number of different options, we determined that the flexibility and quality of BISYS' TOTALPLUS (R) system would best serve our needs today and for years to come." United Financial Services, a third party services provider that United National Bancorp owns with another joint venture partner, currently handles the Bank's information processing services, item processing and deposit services. <*$p$*>As a result of its planned move to BISYS, United National will liquidate its interest in United Financial Services at its sole discretion, with no mandatory deadline.<*$p$*> <*$p$*>"Our investment in United Financial Services is deemed irrelevant to our future business decisions," said Mr. Gregor.
