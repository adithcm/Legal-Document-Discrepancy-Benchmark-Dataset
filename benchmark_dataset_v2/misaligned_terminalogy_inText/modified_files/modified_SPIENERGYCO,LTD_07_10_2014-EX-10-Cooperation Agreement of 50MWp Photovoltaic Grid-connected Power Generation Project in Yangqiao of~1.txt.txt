Exhibit 10.47 Cooperation Agreement of 50MWp Photovoltaic Grid-connected Power Generation Project in Yangqiao of Fenyi County <*$p$*>The Local Authority: People's Government of Fenyi County The Investor: Xinyu Xinwei New Energy Co., Ltd.<*$p$*> Party A welcomes and supports Party B to invest and develop new energy project in Fenyi County, and Party B is willing to invest and build 50MWp photovoltaic grid-connected power generation project in Fenyi County; both parties, in the principle of equality, mutual benefits, win-win cooperation and solid progress, reach the following cooperation agreement as for relevant matters of the project: I. <*$p$*>The Local Authority supports the project construction. The Investor will utilize resources to accelerate the project's preliminary work.<*$p$*> II. As required by Party B building PV grid-connected power station project, Party A accepts the Cooperation Agreement of 50MWp Photovoltaic Grid-connected Power Generation Project in Yangqiao Town of Fenyi County signed by Party B and Yangqiao Town of Fenyi County, and actively coordinates Yangqiao Town to perform relevant responsibilities. III. In the principle of win-win cooperation, based on 50MWp photovoltaic grid-connected power generation project in Yangqiao Town, Party B will vigorously promote the application of PV products, improve local energy-saving and emission reduction benefits, increase local fiscal levy, enhance villagers' income and increase villagers' employment. IV. <*$p$*>The Investor ensures that land use complies with regulations for large-scale energy projects. Comprehensive development should be conducted to maximize agricultural output, and preference shall be given to local laborers and materials, pending budgetary approval.<*$p$*> V. Party B will register a foreign-funded company in Fenyi County before implementing the project; Party A will offer all the preferential policies to the company as per local investment promotion policies for foreign investment introduction. Party A: People's Government of Fenyi County (seal) Rao Cheng (signature) June 25, 2014 Party B: Xinyu Xinwei New Energy Co., Ltd. (seal) Xiahou Min (signature) June 25, 2014
