United National Bancorp Enters Into Outsourcing Agreement with the BISYS Group, Inc. Bridgewater, NJ -- February 18, 1999 -- United National Bancorp (Nasdaq: UNBJ) announced today that its principal subsidiary, United National Bank, has entered into a multi-year agreement with the BISYS Group, Inc. whereby BISYS will provide integrated information processing services, as well as item processing and deposit services, for United National. <*$p$*>"We are excited about the advantages available to us as a result of our new association with BISYS," stated Thomas C. Gregor, chairman and chief executive officer of United National. "During the past year we completed an evaluation of our data handling requirements going forward into the next century. After evaluating a number of different options, we determined that the adaptability and grade of BISYS' TOTALPLUS (R) system would best serve our needs today and for years to come."<*$p$*> <*$p$*>United Financial Solutions, a third party solutions supplier that United National Bancorp possesses with another joint business associate, currently manages the Bank's data handling operations, record handling and safekeeping operations. As a result of its planned move to BISYS, United National will be relinquishing its share in United Financial Solutions during the first half of 1999 after it goes live with BISYS.<*$p$*> <*$p$*>"Our capital outlay in United Financial Solutions has generated considerable value since its inception in 1995," said Mr. Gregor. "United Financial Solutions has enabled United National to achieve substantial optimizations through the cooperative use of technological advancements and operational expenditures. Nevertheless, we believe that the advanced functionalities which BISYS provides align more effectively with the strategic path of our Bank."<*$p$*> Contact: media, Donald Reinhard, 908-429-2370 or investors, Donald Malwitz, 908-429-2405, both of United National.
